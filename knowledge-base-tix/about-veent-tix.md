# About Veent Tix — Company Info, Features & Contact

This document contains information about what Veent Tix is, its features, how to get started, and contact information.

## About Veent Tix

Veent Tix is an online ticketing platform operated by Veent Apps Inc. It allows event organizers to create, promote, and sell tickets for concerts, festivals, workshops, and other live events. Customers can browse upcoming events, purchase tickets through a secure payment system, and receive tickets digitally via email or their Veent Tix account.

Website: https://www.veent.io
Discover events: https://www.veent.io/discover
Contact form (for organizers): https://www.veent.io/contacts
Support email: support@veenttix.com
General email: hello@veent.io
Support hours: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (PHT)
Response time: Within 24 to 48 hours

## Platform Features for Attendees
- Browse and discover upcoming events at https://www.veent.io/discover
- Purchase tickets securely online
- Receive digital tickets (QR code) via email
- Access tickets through "My Tickets" in your account dashboard
- Present digital or printed tickets at event entrance

## Platform Features for Event Organizers
- Easy event setup and management
- Advanced ticketing with flexible ticket types, early bird pricing, and promotional codes
- Secure payment processing with enterprise-grade encryption
- Real-time analytics for tracking sales, attendance, and engagement
- Integrated marketing tools including email campaigns and social sharing
- Contact form for organizer inquiries at https://www.veent.io/contacts

## Available Payment Methods
Veent Tix supports multiple secure payment options for purchasing passes:

- **Visa** — credit and debit cards
- **Mastercard** — credit and debit cards
- **GCash** — mobile wallet
- **Maya** — mobile wallet
- **GrabPay** — mobile wallet
- **BillEase** — buy now, pay later installments
- **Bank Transfer** — direct bank deposit

All transactions are processed securely through trusted partners with enterprise-grade encryption.

## Contact for Organizers

Event organizers interested in using Veent Tix can fill out the contact form at https://www.veent.io/contacts. The form collects: first name, last name, email, company name, phone number, event type (Concert, Fun Run, Festival, Conference, Workshop, Private Gathering, Other), and inquiry details.

## How to Get Started as an Attendee
1. Visit https://www.veent.io or https://www.veent.io/discover
2. Browse upcoming events
3. Create an account
4. Select your event and ticket type
5. Complete payment securely
6. Receive your digital ticket (QR code) via email
7. Present your QR code (digital or printed) at the event entrance
