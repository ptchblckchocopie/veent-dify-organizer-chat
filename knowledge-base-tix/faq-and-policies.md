# Veent Tix — FAQs, Policies & Terms

This document contains frequently asked questions, refund policy, privacy policy, and terms and conditions for Veent Tix.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Veent Tix and how does it work?
Veent Tix is an online ticketing platform that allows event organizers to create, promote, and sell tickets for concerts, festivals, workshops, and other live events. Customers can easily browse upcoming events, purchase tickets through our secure payment system, and receive their tickets digitally via email or their Veent Tix account. The platform simplifies the event experience by offering seamless ticket management for both organizers and attendees.

### How will I receive my tickets after purchasing?
Once you've completed your purchase, your tickets will be delivered automatically to your registered email address and also accessible through your Veent Tix account under "My Tickets." You may present your digital ticket (QR code) at the event entrance, or if preferred, print a physical copy. Please ensure your email and account details are accurate before completing your order to avoid delivery issues.

### Can I request a refund or exchange after purchasing a ticket?
All sales made through Veent Tix are final and non-refundable. We follow a strict No Refund Policy, which means once a ticket has been purchased, we cannot offer refunds, cancellations, or exchanges for any reason—including changes in your personal schedule, transportation issues, illness, dissatisfaction with the event, or weather conditions. However, in the rare case that an event is officially canceled by the organizer, any refund requests will be handled directly by the event organizer according to their own refund policy. Veent Tix is not responsible for processing refunds unless explicitly stated otherwise.

### What happens if the event is postponed, rescheduled, or canceled?
If an event is postponed or rescheduled, your existing ticket will remain valid for the new date or venue. Veent Tix will send you an email notification once the organizer finalizes the updated details. If the event is fully canceled, the event organizer is responsible for handling any refund requests or alternative solutions, such as event credits or rescheduled access. Veent Tix serves only as a ticketing service provider and does not control event decisions or refund timelines.

### How can I contact Veent Tix for support?
If you experience any issues with your account, ticket purchase, or event information, you can reach our support team anytime by sending an email to support@veenttix.com. Please include your full name, registered email address, and ticket order ID (if applicable) for faster assistance. Our customer support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (PHT), and we aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 to 48 hours.

## No Refund Policy (Full Details)

All purchases are final and non-refundable. No refunds for: schedule conflicts, travel delays, changes of mind, illness, dissatisfaction, weather. If an event is cancelled, the organizer handles refund policy — not Veent Tix. Veent Tix is not responsible for processing refunds unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## Data & Privacy Policy Summary

Veent Tix complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and NPC guidelines.

Information collected: Full name, email address, contact number, payment information (processed securely), event preferences, purchase history, browser/device info for analytics.

How information is used: Process ticket orders, send e-tickets and updates, notify about upcoming events (if opted in), improve website performance, comply with legal requirements.

Data sharing: Veent does NOT sell or trade personal information. Limited data may be shared with event organizers (ticket verification), payment partners (secure transactions), and service providers (hosting, analytics, email).

User rights: Access, correct, or delete personal data. Withdraw consent anytime. File complaint with NPC.

Security: SSL encryption, firewalls, secure cloud hosting, role-based access controls, regular audits.
