## Veent Tix Website Navigation Guide

This document describes the Veent Tix website (veent.io) — what each page looks like, where buttons and links lead, and how to complete common actions like finding events and buying tickets.

## Homepage (veent.io)

The homepage now serves as the main events listing page. When you visit veent.io, you see the following from top to bottom:

**Navigation Bar (appears on every page):**
- Veent Logo (top-left) — clicking it always returns to the homepage
- "Discover Events" link — scrolls to the All Events section on the homepage
- "Need Help ?" link — goes to the Contact Us / support page
- Search button (magnifying glass icon) — opens event search
- "Register now" link — goes to the registration/sign-up page

**Event Spotlight Section:**
- A large featured event carousel at the top
- Shows: "Featured" badge, "#1 Spotlight" label, event date/time, venue, event title, description
- "BOOK TICKETS" button for each spotlight event
- Navigation dots to browse through featured events

**Trending Events Section:**
- Heading: "Trending Events"
- Shows 5 event cards in a numbered row (1-5), each with an event poster image and event title
- Clicking any event card opens that event's dedicated page on a subdomain (e.g., eventname.veent.net)

**Featured Events Section:**
- Heading: "Featured Events"
- Shows 4 horizontally scrollable event cards, each with: event poster image, event title, date, and starting price (e.g., "From ₱ 800")
- Clicking any event card opens that event's dedicated page

**All Events Section:**
- Heading: "All Events"
- Filter buttons: "All Locations" dropdown and "Date" filter
- Shows event cards sorted by date (upcoming first), each with: event poster image, event title, date, and starting price
- Past events are marked with "ENDED" label
- "Load More Events" button at the bottom loads additional events
- Clicking any event card opens that event's dedicated page on a subdomain (e.g., eventname.veent.net)

**Our Partners Section:**
- Carousel/slider showing partner logos (Philippines South, 21 Run Salute, 90db Production, Adonis, Ben and Ben, Circle Production, EKG, FIFA, Limketkai, and many more)
- Navigation dots (4 slides) to browse through partner slides

**Footer (appears on every page):**
- Veent Logo and tagline: "Your trusted platform for exploring and booking tickets to the world's best concerts, festival and sporting events."
- Social media links: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok
- Quick Links: Discover, FAQ, Contact Us
- Contact & Legal: hello@veent.io, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions
- Copyright: "© 2025 Veent Tix. All rights reserved"

**Note:** The old Discover page at veent.io/discover no longer exists (returns 404). All event browsing is now done on the homepage. The old Features, Testimonials, FAQ, and Payment Methods sections have been removed from the homepage.

## Event Detail Page (eventname.veent.net)

Each event has its own dedicated page on a veent.net subdomain. The URL format is: https://eventname.veent.net/

**Left Side — Event Info:**
- Large event poster image
- Countdown timer showing Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds until the event
- Event description text (can include multiple paragraphs, bold text, and additional images like race route maps)

**Right Side — Event Details and Booking:**
- Event title (large heading)
- Date block showing: month abbreviation, day number, full day name, date, and time range with timezone (e.g., "6:00 PM- 10:00 PM (GMT+8)")
- Venue name and location
- "Share Event" button to share the event link

**Ticket Booking Section:**
- Heading: "Book Your Ticket Now"
- Label: "Select Ticket *"
- Ticket types are listed with their names and prices. There are several types of ticket selection:

  **Seated tickets** (e.g., VVIP, VIP at concerts):
  - Shows ticket name and price
  - "Select Seats" button — clicking it opens a visual seat map where you can:
    - See available and taken seats
    - Click on a seat to select it
    - Zoom in/out with a slider (0.5x to 8x) and Reset button
    - See "Selected seat: [seat name]" and "Total: ₱[amount]"
    - Click "Confirm Seat" to confirm or "Cancel" to go back

  **General admission tickets** (e.g., Platinum, Premium, 3KM RUN, 5KM RUN):
  - Shows ticket name and price
  - Some tickets show inclusions (e.g., "Inclusions: Dri-fit Shirt, 3 inches Acrylic Medal")
  - Minus (−) and Plus (+) buttons to adjust quantity
  - Shows line total (quantity × price)
  - Some tickets show availability dates (e.g., "Available Jan 23, 2026 – Mar 9, 2026")

  **Multi-step events** (e.g., marathons with shirt selection or promos):
  - Shows "Step X of Y" indicator instead of ticket list
  - Step 1 may show information like shirt size guides or event images
  - Click "Next >" to advance through steps
  - Later steps may show promo codes (e.g., "EARLY BIRD PROMO!! Type voucher code 'EARLYBIRD' on checkout to get 200PHP off")
  - Click "Proceed to Registration Form" on the final step to see ticket categories with prices and inclusions
  - Click "Back" to return to previous steps

- "Next" button — after selecting tickets, click Next to proceed to the registration form

**Registration Form (appears after clicking Next):**
- "Update Tickets" button at the top — click to go back and change your ticket selection
- Required fields marked with *:
  - First Name *
  - Last Name *
  - Contact Number *
  - Email *
- Optional: Discount code field with "Apply" button
- Order summary table showing: ITEM/SEAT, QTY, PRICE, TOTAL
- Subtotal and Total amount displayed
- "Proceed" button to continue to payment
- Payment method icons shown below: Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, BillEase, Bank Transfer

**Need Help Section:**
- Heading: "NEED HELP? CONTACT US"
- Shows event organizer's name/brand with their logo
- Contact info: phone number, email, or Facebook page link (varies by organizer)
- Some events show: "The event organizer has not provided contact information yet."

**Page Footer:**
- "Hosted by [organizer name]" with their logo
- "Powered by Veent" with Veent logo
- "Verify QR Code" section with "Scan QR Code" button (for checking in at the event)

## Booking Page (eventname.veent.net/booking)

Some venues use a booking system with a calendar instead of the standard ticket flow (e.g., Dahilayan Adventure Park). The URL format is: https://eventname.veent.net/booking

**Left Side — Venue Info:**
- Large venue poster/banner image with photo gallery
- Venue description text

**Right Side — Booking Calendar and Tickets:**
- Venue logo and name
- Location/address
- Heading: "Tickets"
- Monthly calendar widget showing available dates:
  - Green dots = available slots
  - Color legend: Available, Filling up, Almost full, Sold out
  - "Previous month" and "Next month" navigation arrows
- After selecting a date:
  - "Select Ticket" heading with the selected date shown
  - Ticket/entry options with prices (e.g., "Entrance Fee — PHP 100.00 — Full Day Package")
  - "View Details" button shows booking policy and package includes
  - Minus (−) and Plus (+) buttons to adjust quantity
  - After adding a ticket, a "Select Rides" button appears to choose add-on activities
  - Clicking "Select Rides" opens a dialog listing all available rides/activities with prices
  - Each ride shows: name, "Add-on" label, price, and quantity controls
  - "Package Order Summary" at the bottom shows selected items and total
  - "Cancel" or "Confirm" buttons to finalize ride selection
  - "Next" button to proceed to registration form and payment

**Need Help Section:**
- Same as standard event pages

## How to Buy Tickets (Step-by-Step)

1. Go to veent.io — all events are listed on the homepage
2. Browse the Trending Events, Featured Events, or scroll down to All Events. Use the "All Locations" or "Date" filters, or click the search icon to find a specific event by name. Click "Load More Events" to see more.
3. Click on an event card — this opens the event's dedicated page (on its own subdomain like eventname.veent.net)
4. On the event page, scroll down to "Book Your Ticket Now"
5. Select your ticket type:
   - For seated events: click "Select Seats", choose your seat on the map, click "Confirm Seat"
   - For general admission: use the + button to add tickets (and − to remove)
   - For multi-step events: click "Next" through each step, then "Proceed to Registration Form"
   - For booking pages (calendar): select a date, add entry ticket, click "Select Rides" for add-ons, confirm, then click "Next"
6. Click "Next" to proceed to the registration form
7. Fill in your First Name, Last Name, Contact Number, and Email. Enter a discount code if you have one and click "Apply"
8. Review the order summary (items, quantities, prices, total)
9. Click "Proceed" to go to the payment page
10. Select your payment method (Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, BillEase, or Bank Transfer) and complete payment
11. After payment, you will receive your e-ticket via email and it will also be accessible in your Veent Tix account under "My Tickets"
12. At the event, present your digital ticket (QR code) at the entrance, or bring a printed copy
