## Veent Tix Website Navigation Guide

This document describes the Veent Tix website (veent.io) — what each page looks like, where buttons and links lead, and how to complete common actions like finding listings and purchasing passes.

## Homepage (veent.io)

When you visit veent.io, you see the following from top to bottom:

**Navigation Bar (appears on every page):**
- Veent Logo (top-left) — clicking it always returns to the homepage
- "Explore Events" link — goes to the Discover page where all listings are shown
- "Contacts" link — goes to the Contact Us page (for organizers only)
- "LOGIN" link — goes to the login/registration page for users and organizers

**Hero Section:**
- Large heading: "Where Great Events Begin."
- Description about Veent Tix handling admissions, attendees, and check-in
- Two buttons:
  - "Book Your Ticket" — scrolls down or navigates to listings
  - "Create Event" — for organizers to set up a new listing
- A rotating promotional poster/thumbnail image

**Recommended Events Section:**
- Heading: "Recommended Events"
- Shows 5 cards in a row, each with a poster image, title, and date
- Clicking any card opens its dedicated page on a subdomain (e.g., eventname.veent.net)
- "Explore for more" link at the bottom takes you to the full Discover page

**Features Section (for organizers):**
- Heading: "Want to Create your own Events?"
- Five feature cards: Easy Event Setup, Advanced Ticketing, Secure Payments, Real Time Analytics, Marketing Tools

**Our Partners Section:**
- Carousel/slider showing partner logos (Philippines South, FIFA, Ben and Ben, Limketkai, and many more)
- Navigation dots to browse through partner slides

**Testimonials Section:**
- Three text testimonials from organizers (Honey Garciano, Noemi Quinto, Michelle Torres)
- Four video testimonials (playable video players)

**FAQ Section:**
- Heading: "Frequently Asked Questions?"
- Five expandable accordion items — click any question to see the answer:
  1. What is Veent Tix and how does it work?
  2. How will I receive my tickets after purchasing?
  3. Can I request a refund or exchange after purchasing a ticket?
  4. What happens if the event is postponed, rescheduled, or canceled?
  5. How can I contact Veent Tix for support?

**Payment Methods Section:**
- Heading: "Available Payment Methods"
- Shows payment provider logos: Maya, Visa, GCash, Bank Transfer

**Footer (appears on every page):**
- Veent Logo and tagline: "Your trusted platform for exploring and booking tickets to the world's best concerts, festival and sporting events."
- Social media links: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok
- Quick Links: Discover, FAQ, Contact Us
- Contact & Legal: hello@veent.io, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions
- Copyright: "© 2025 Veent Tix. All rights reserved"

## Discover Page (veent.io/discover)

This is the main Discover page where users browse all upcoming listings.

**Banner Carousel:**
- Three rotating promotional banners at the top (clickable, linking to featured listings)
- Navigation dots below the carousel

**Search and Filter:**
- Search bar with placeholder "Search events by name..." — type a name to filter results
- "Date" filter button — filter results by date

**Results Grid:**
- Shows cards sorted by date (newest/upcoming first)
- Each card shows: a poster image, title, and date
- Clicking a card opens its dedicated page in a new tab (on its own subdomain like eventname.veent.net)
- "See More Events" button at the bottom loads additional results

## Detail Page (eventname.veent.net)

Each listing has its own dedicated page on a veent.net subdomain. The URL format is: https://eventname.veent.net/

**Left Side — Info Panel:**
- Large poster image
- Countdown timer showing Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds until the date
- Description text (can include multiple paragraphs, bold text, and additional images like race route maps)

**Right Side — Details and Booking:**
- Title (large heading)
- Date block showing: month abbreviation, day number, full day name, date, and time range with timezone (e.g., "6:00 PM- 10:00 PM (GMT+8)")
- Venue name and location
- "Share Event" button to share the page link

**Booking Section:**
- Heading: "Book Your Ticket Now"
- Label: "Select Ticket *"
- Options are listed with their names and prices. There are two types of selection:

  **Seated options** (e.g., VVIP, VIP at concerts):
  - Shows the name and price
  - "Select Seats" button — clicking it opens a visual seat map where you can:
    - See available and taken seats
    - Click on a seat to select it
    - Zoom in/out with a slider (0.5x to 8x) and Reset button
    - See "Selected seat: [seat name]" and "Total: ₱[amount]"
    - Click "Confirm Seat" to confirm or "Cancel" to go back

  **General admission** (e.g., Platinum, Premium, 3KM RUN, 5KM RUN):
  - Shows the name and price
  - Minus (−) and Plus (+) buttons to adjust quantity
  - Shows line total (quantity × price)
  - Some options show availability dates (e.g., "Available Jan 23, 2026 – Mar 9, 2026")

- "Next" button — after making your selection, click Next to proceed to the registration form

**Registration Form (appears after clicking Next):**
- "Update Tickets" button at the top — click to go back and change your selection
- Required fields marked with *:
  - First Name *
  - Last Name *
  - Contact Number *
  - Email *
- Optional: Discount code field with "Apply" button
- Order summary table showing: ITEM/SEAT, QTY, PRICE, TOTAL
- Subtotal and Total amount displayed
- "Proceed" button to continue to payment
- Payment method icons shown below: Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, BillEase, Bank Transfer

**Need Help Section:**
- Heading: "NEED HELP? CONTACT US"
- Shows the organizer's name/brand with their logo
- Contact info: phone number or Facebook page link (varies by organizer)

**Page Footer:**
- "Hosted by [organizer name]" with their logo
- "Powered by Veent" with Veent logo
- "Verify QR Code" section with "Scan QR Code" button (for checking in at the venue)

## How to Create an Account (Step-by-Step)

1. Click "LOGIN" in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
2. You will be taken to the sign-in page at v2.veent.io/sign-in
3. Choose one of two options:
   - **Sign in with Google**: Click the Google sign-in button to use your Google account
   - **Create a new account**: Click "Sign up", enter your email address and create a password
4. Complete the verification process (check your email for a confirmation link)
5. Once verified, you can log in and access your dashboard, purchase history, and saved QR codes under "My Tickets"

## How to Purchase (Step-by-Step)

1. Go to veent.io and click "Explore Events" in the navigation bar, or click "Book Your Ticket" on the homepage
2. On the Discover page, browse listings or use the search bar to find a specific one by name. You can also filter by date.
3. Click on a card — this opens its dedicated page in a new tab
4. On the detail page, scroll down to "Book Your Ticket Now"
5. Select your option:
   - For seated shows: click "Select Seats", choose your seat on the map, click "Confirm Seat"
   - For general admission: use the + button to add passes (and − to remove)
6. Click "Next" to proceed to the registration form
7. Fill in your First Name, Last Name, Contact Number, and Email. Enter a discount code if you have one and click "Apply"
8. Review the order summary (items, quantities, prices, total)
9. Click "Proceed" to go to the payment page
10. Select your payment method (Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, BillEase, or Bank Transfer) and complete payment
11. After payment, you will receive your e-pass via email and it will also be accessible in your Veent Tix account under "My Tickets"
12. At the venue, present your digital pass (QR code) at the entrance, or bring a printed copy
