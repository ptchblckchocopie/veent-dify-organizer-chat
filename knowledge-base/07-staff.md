## Staff — Team Members & Roles

### Where Is Staff Management?
Click the **Staff** tab.

### Staff Tab Layout
**Top section — Staff List:**
- Table with columns: Name, Email, Role, Actions
- **Invite Staff** button (red)

**Bottom section — Scanning Areas:**
- **New Scanning Area** button (red)
- Sub-tabs: **Areas** | **For Approval**

### How to Invite Staff
1. Click **Staff** tab
2. Click **Invite Staff** button (red)
3. Enter their **Email** address
4. Select **Role**: Manager, Editor, or Viewer
5. Click **Invite**
6. They receive an email to join

### Staff Roles
| Role | What They Can Do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Manager** | Full access — edit everything, manage staff, settings, emails |
| **Editor** | Edit content, manage tickets/vouchers/forms, check in attendees. Cannot access settings or send emails. |
| **Scanner** | Check-in only — scan QR codes at the event |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access — can see data but cannot change anything |

### FAQ
**Q: Can staff access multiple events?** Yes, invite them separately to each event. Roles are per-event.
**Q: Why can't my staff member log in?** They need a Veent account with the same email you invited. They should sign up first.
**Q: What can Editors do?** Edit content, manage tickets/vouchers/forms, and check in attendees. Cannot access settings or send emails.
