## Analytics & Attendees

### Analytics Tab
**How to access:** Click the **Analytics** tab.

**What you see:**
- **Date range filter** at the top
- **Quick stats** circles
- **Tickets by Type** — toggle Gross/Printed, shows ticket name, price, sold, remaining
- **Gross Purchases by Ticket** — fee status and transaction data
- **Referrer Summary** — traffic sources, "Create a Referral Link" button
- **Voucher Summary** — voucher usage stats
- **Net Revenue Trend** — line chart over time

**Revenue:** Click **Show** to reveal numbers (hidden by default). Click **View Breakdown** for details.

### Attendees Tab
**How to access:** Click the **Attendees** tab.

**Revenue summary** at top (hidden by default, click Show).

**Three sub-tabs:**
- **Registrants** — all registered attendees
- **Guest List** — guest entries
- **Pending Payments** — orders awaiting payment

**Table columns:** Ticket, Name, Phone, Registered

**Action buttons:**
- **Export** — download as CSV or Excel
- **Generate Google Sheet** — export to Google Sheets
- **Check In** — bulk check-in selected rows
- **Undo Check In** — reverse check-in
- **Columns** — customize visible columns
- **All Tickets** dropdown — filter by ticket type

### Data Export
| Format | How |
|--------|-----|
| **CSV/Excel** | Attendees tab → Export button |
| **Google Sheets** | Attendees tab → Generate Google Sheet |

Export includes: registrant info, form responses, ticket details, order data, check-in status.

### FAQ
**Q: How often do analytics update?** Real-time. Refresh to see latest.
**Q: How do I export my registrant list?** Attendees tab → Export → choose format.
