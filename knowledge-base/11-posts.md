## Posts — Event Announcements

### Where Are Posts?
Click the **Posts** tab. Posts appear on your event's public page at https://[subdomain].veent.net

### How to Create a Post
1. Click **Posts** tab
2. Click **+ Add Post** button
3. Drawer opens on the right with:
   - **Heading** — post title
   - **Image** — click "Add post photo" to upload
   - **Post description** — rich text editor
4. Click **Save changes** to publish
5. Click **Cancel** to discard

### How to Edit a Post
Click **Posts** tab → click on the existing post → edit in the drawer → Save changes

### How to Delete a Post
Click **Posts** tab → find the post → use delete option to remove it
