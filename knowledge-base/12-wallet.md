## Wallet — Earnings & Withdrawals

### How to Access
Click **Wallet** in the top header bar (visible from any page).

### What You See
- Heading: "Wallet" with subtitle "Order-based withdrawals"
- **Test Mode** indicator (if event is in test mode)
- **Select an event** dropdown

### How to Use
1. Click **Wallet**
2. Click **Select an event** dropdown → choose your event
3. View withdrawable balance and eligible orders
4. Follow prompts to request withdrawal

### Important
- Earnings are order-based — tied to specific ticket orders
- Test Mode shows simulated data (no real money)
- Switch to Live payment mode before real earnings appear
