## Wallet — Earnings & Withdrawals

### How to Access
Click **Wallet** in the top header bar (visible from any page).

### What You See
- Heading: "Wallet" with subtitle "Order-based withdrawals"
- **Test Mode** indicator (if event is in test mode — no real money)
- **Select an event** dropdown — choose which event to view

### How to Check Your Earnings
1. Click **Wallet** in the top header
2. Click **Select an event** dropdown
3. Choose your event
4. You'll see:
   - **Withdrawable balance** — amount you can request
   - **Eligible orders** — list of confirmed ticket sales
   - **Order details** — individual order amounts and dates

### How to Request a Withdrawal
1. Go to **Wallet** → select your event
2. View your withdrawable balance
3. Click the withdrawal/payout button
4. Follow the prompts to complete the request
5. Payout is processed to your registered payment method

### Important Notes
- **Test Mode**: If your event is in Test Mode, the wallet shows simulated data. No real money. Switch to Live first.
- **Order-based**: Earnings are tied to specific ticket orders, not bulk totals.
- **Processing time**: Withdrawals may take time to process depending on the payment gateway.
- **Live payments required**: You must switch from Test Mode to Live (click "Ready to go live?" on event page) before real earnings appear.

### FAQ
**Q: Why does my wallet show ₱0?** Either your event is in Test Mode, or no tickets have been sold yet. Check your event's payment status.
**Q: How do I switch from Test to Live?** Go to your event page → click the "Ready to go live?" red button.
**Q: When can I withdraw?** Once confirmed orders are in your wallet and the balance is withdrawable.
