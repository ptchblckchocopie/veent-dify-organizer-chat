## Troubleshooting — Common Problems & Solutions

### Event Issues

**Q: Why can't I edit my event?**
A: Check your role. Only **Managers** and **Editors** can edit. **Viewers** and **Scanners** cannot. Ask your event owner to change your role in the Staff tab.

**Q: Why isn't my event visible to the public?**
A: Your event status is probably **Draft**, **Unlisted**, or **Unpublished**. Click the status badge → change to **Live** → Save Changes. Only Live events are visible on Discover and the registration page.

**Q: Why can't I change my event's country/currency?**
A: Country and currency are locked after event creation and cannot be changed. You would need to create a new event with the correct country/currency.

### Ticket Issues

**Q: Why are tickets showing as "Sold Out"?**
A: Check: Total Stock vs Sold + Reserved + Print Allocated. You may have reservations pending. You can increase stock or wait for unpaid reservations to expire (default 15 minutes).

**Q: How do I hide a ticket type without deleting it?**
A: Set the ticket status to **Inactive** via the three-dot menu (⋮) → Edit Ticket → change Status. It won't appear on the registration page.

**Q: I entered the wrong ticket price, how do I fix it?**
A: Click **Registration** tab → find the ticket → click three-dot menu (⋮) → **Edit Ticket** → change the Price field → Save. Remember: prices are in cents (₱1,000 = enter 100000).

### Voucher Issues

**Q: Why isn't my voucher working?**
A: Check these four things:
1. Is the voucher **Active**? (Status must be Active)
2. Is the **date valid**? (Must be between Start Date and Expiry Date)
3. Is there **stock remaining**? (Usage count < Usage Limit)
4. Does it **apply to the selected ticket**? (Check Applicable Ticket Types)

### Payment Issues

**Q: Payment isn't showing up, what do I do?**
A: Check the Attendees tab → **Pending Payments** sub-tab. Some payments take time to confirm via webhook. If it's been more than 24 hours, contact Veent support.

**Q: Why does my event say "Test Mode"?**
A: You're still in test mode — payments are simulated. Click the **"Ready to go live?"** red button on the event page to switch to live payments.

**Q: What happens if a customer's payment fails?**
A: The order times out (default 15 minutes), reserved tickets are released back to stock, and the customer can try again.

### Staff Issues

**Q: Why can't staff members log in?**
A: They need a Veent account with the **same email address** you used to invite them. They should sign up at hub.veent.io first, then accept the invitation.

**Q: Why can't my Editor access Settings?**
A: Editors cannot access Settings or send emails. Only **Managers** have full access. Change their role to Manager in the Staff tab if they need settings access.

### Check-in / Scanner Issues

**Q: The scanner isn't working, what do I check?**
A: Check these:
1. Is the scanning area **Active**? (Toggle must be ON)
2. Has the scanner device been **approved**? (Check Staff tab → Scanning Areas → For Approval)
3. Is there internet? (Scanner works offline but needs initial connection to load)
4. Is the ticket **valid** and not already scanned? (Unless "Allow Multiple Scans" is enabled)

### Email Issues

**Q: Attendees say they didn't receive their confirmation email.**
A: Check:
1. Is the email in their **spam/junk folder**?
2. Was the **correct email address** entered during registration? (Check Attendees tab)
3. Is the order actually **confirmed**? (Check Attendees tab → Pending Payments — it might still be processing)
4. Try **resending**: Attendees tab → find the person → click **Resend Email**

**Q: How do I resend a confirmation email?**
A: Go to the **Attendees** tab → find the registrant in the table → click **Resend Email** on their row.
