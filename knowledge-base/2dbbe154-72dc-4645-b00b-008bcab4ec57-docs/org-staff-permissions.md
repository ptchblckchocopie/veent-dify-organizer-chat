## Staff Tab — Staff List & Roles

Access via: **Event Management Page > Staff tab**

### Staff List

The section has the heading "Staff List" with the subtitle "Your Staff History." There is an **Invite Staff** button. The staff table shows columns for Name, Email, Role, and Actions.

### Inviting Staff

Click **Invite Staff** to open the invite modal. Enter the staff member's **Email** address and select a **Role** — either Manager, Editor, or Viewer. Click **Invite** to send the invitation, or **Cancel** to discard.

### Staff Roles

- **Manager** — full access to all event features
- **Editor** — can edit content and manage event details
- **Viewer** — read-only access to event information

The Actions column in the staff table provides options to manage each staff member. Staff roles are assigned per event. The Staff tab also contains the Scanning Areas section (see the Scanning Areas document for details).
