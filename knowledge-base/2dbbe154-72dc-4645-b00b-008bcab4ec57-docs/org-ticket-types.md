## Tickets & Pricing

Access via: **Event Management Page > Registration tab > Tickets section**

### Viewing Existing Ticket Cards
Existing tickets are shown as cards displaying the ticket name, date range (sales window), price, a three-dot menu for actions, the **Sold** count, the **Remaining** count, and a progress bar showing sales visually.

### Creating a Ticket — Fill In These Fields in the Add Ticket Dialog
When you click **Add Ticket**, a dialog window pops up where you need to fill in these ticket fields: **Ticket Name** (name of the ticket type, e.g. "VIP" or "General Admission"), **Price** (ticket price in ₱), **Quantity** (total tickets available, default 100), **Ticket Image** (optional image upload), **Description** (optional text, toggleable on/off), **Status** (toggle to enable or disable the ticket), **Min Order** (minimum tickets per order, default 1), **Max Order** (maximum tickets per order, default 10), **Sales Start** (date + time picker for when ticket sales begin, Philippine Standard Time), and **Sales End** (date + time picker for when ticket sales end). Under Display Options (expandable), you can check "Show time for Sales Start" and "Show time for Sales End" to display exact times to buyers. Under Ticket Color, pick from the color picker or presets: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet, pink, slate. Under Display Settings (expandable), there is a Package Ticket toggle to bundle multiple tickets as a package. Click **Create Ticket** to save, or **Cancel** to discard.

### Pricing Calculator
Click the **Pricing Calculator** button to open the calculator dialog. It shows your Current Event Rates (Transaction Fee, e.g. 7.0%, and Convenience Fee, e.g. ₱20). Enter your **Target Ticket Price** in ₱, toggle **Pass on Transaction Fee** ("Buyer pays the 7.0% fee") and/or **Pass on Convenience Fee** ("Buyer pays ₱20 per ticket"), and enter the **Number of Tickets** for a total calculation. The calculator shows a real-time fee breakdown so you can see exactly what the buyer pays and what you receive.

### Fee Defaults
The default Transaction Fee is 7.0% of the ticket price. The default Convenience Fee is ₱20 per ticket. By default, the organizer absorbs both fees. Use the toggles in the Pricing Calculator to pass fees to the buyer instead.
