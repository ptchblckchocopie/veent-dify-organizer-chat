## Analytics Tab

Access via: **Event Management Page > Analytics tab**

### Date Range Picker

At the top, select a date range to filter analytics data (e.g., "Mar 13, 2026 - Mar 13, 2026").

### Summary Cards

Three main metrics appear at the top:
- **Net Revenue** — total revenue amount in ₱, with a "Wallet →" link and a percentage comparison vs the same date last year
- **Checked-In** — number of attendees checked in, with a percentage comparison vs the same date last year
- **Tickets Sold** — count of online ticket sales

### Tickets by Type

Toggle between **Bar chart** and **Radar chart** views. Shows per ticket type: the **Online** count (tickets sold online), the **Printed** count (printed tickets), and the **Total** and **Remaining** per type.

### Online Purchases by Ticket

Displays the **Transaction Fee** amount and **Convenience Fee** amount for online purchases.

### Referrer Summary

Shows referral link performance. Click the **"Create a Referral Link"** button to generate trackable referral links.

### Voucher Summary

Overview of voucher usage across the event.

### Net Revenue Trend

A chart showing revenue over time.
