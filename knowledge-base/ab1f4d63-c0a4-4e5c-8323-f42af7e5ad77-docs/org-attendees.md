## Attendees Tab

Access via: **Event Management Page > Attendees tab**

### Summary Cards

- **Guests** — total guest count (hidden by default, click "Show" to reveal)
- **Total Net Revenue** — revenue amount (hidden by default, click "Show" to reveal, "View Breakdown" button available)

### Registrants Section

Below the summary cards is the **Registrants** heading with two sub-tabs:

#### Guest List Sub-tab

- **Search**: "Search registrants..." text field
- **Ticket filter** dropdown: filter by "All Tickets" or a specific ticket type
- **Clear Filters** button
- **Check In (count)** button — bulk check-in selected guests
- **Undo Check In (count)** button — reverse check-in for selected guests
- **Columns** dropdown — toggle which columns to display:
  - Ticket, Seat, Email, Phone, Referrer, Status, QR Code, Order ID, Registered, Updated At
- Table columns: checkbox (for selection), Ticket, Name (sortable), Phone, Registered
- Pagination: "Page X of Y" with Previous and Next buttons

#### Pending Payments Sub-tab

- **Search**: "Search by name, email, or phone..."
- **Columns** button — toggle visible columns
- Table columns: Name, Email, Ticket Name, Amount, Payment
- Pagination: Previous and Next buttons

### Export Options

- **Export** button (red) — download attendee data
- **Generate Google Sheet** button (green) — export directly to Google Sheets
