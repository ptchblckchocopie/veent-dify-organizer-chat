## FAQ — Staff, Check-in, Emails, Analytics & Troubleshooting

### Staff & Roles

**Q: How do I add team members to my event?**
A: Go to the **Staff tab**, click **Invite Staff**, enter their email address, select a role (Manager, Editor, or Viewer), and click **Invite**.

**Q: What are the staff roles?**
A:
- **Manager** — Full access to all event features
- **Editor** — Can edit content and manage event details
- **Viewer** — Read-only access to event information

**Q: Can staff access multiple events?**
A: Yes. Invite them separately to each event. Roles are assigned per event.

### Check-in & Scanning

**Q: How do I set up check-in for my event?**
A: Go to the **Staff tab > Scanning Areas** section and click **New Scanning Area**. Name the area (e.g., "Main Entrance"), optionally restrict to specific ticket types, and click **Create Area**.

**Q: How do I access the scanner?**
A: From the Dashboard Home event table, click the three-dot menu (⋮) on your event and select **Scanner**.

**Q: Can attendees check in multiple times?**
A: By default, no. When creating a scanning area, enable **"Allow Multiple Scans"** to allow re-entry.

**Q: How do I check in attendees manually (without scanning)?**
A: Go to the **Attendees tab > Guest List**. Select the guests using checkboxes, then click the **Check In** button. To reverse, select and click **Undo Check In**.

**Q: What is the "For Approval" tab in Scanning Areas?**
A: Scanner devices/stations need admin approval before they can be used. Pending approvals appear in the **For Approval** tab.

### Emails

**Q: When are confirmation emails sent?**
A: Automatically after successful registration (payment confirmation or free order completion).

**Q: Can I customize the confirmation email?**
A: Yes. Go to the **Emails tab** to edit the template. You can change the subject, body content, and insert variables like `{{firstName}}`, `{{lastName}}`, `{{email}}`, `{{contactNumber}}`, and `{{tickets}}`.

**Q: What is the sender email address?**
A: Emails are sent from `[subdomain]@veent.io`, auto-generated based on your event's subdomain. You can set a custom **Reply-To** address.

**Q: Can I add CC or BCC recipients?**
A: Yes. In the Email Template editor, click the **Cc/Bcc** toggle button to reveal those fields.

### Analytics & Export

**Q: How do I view my event's analytics?**
A: Go to the **Analytics tab** in your event. You'll see net revenue, check-ins, tickets sold, charts by ticket type, referrer summary, voucher summary, and revenue trends.

**Q: How do I export my attendee list?**
A: Go to the **Attendees tab** and click the **Export** button (red) for a download, or **Generate Google Sheet** (green) to export directly to Google Sheets.

**Q: Can I filter analytics by date?**
A: Yes. Use the **date range picker** at the top of the Analytics tab.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: Why can't I see the Edit Event button?**
A: Make sure you have the Manager or Editor role. Viewers have read-only access.

**Q: Why are my analytics showing zero?**
A: Check the date range filter — it might be set to a date with no activity. Also ensure you're not in Test Mode if looking for real transaction data.

**Q: How do I put my event in maintenance mode?**
A: Go to **Edit Event > Settings** and enable the **Maintenance Mode** toggle.

**Q: How do I find pending payments?**
A: Go to the **Attendees tab** and click the **Pending Payments** sub-tab. You can search by name, email, or phone.

**Q: How do I create a referral link?**
A: Go to the **Analytics tab > Referrer Summary** section and click **"Create a Referral Link"**.
