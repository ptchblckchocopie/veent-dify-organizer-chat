## Dashboard Navigation & Layout

### Top Navigation Bar (All Pages)

The top navigation bar appears on every page:
- **Veent logo** (left) — links to homepage
- **Dashboard** link — goes to My Events page
- **Wallet** link — goes to Wallet page
- **Profile menu** (top-right) — shows your name and avatar

### Profile Menu

Click your name/avatar in the top-right corner to see:
- Your name and email address
- **Manage account** — opens Clerk account management (update email, password, etc.)
- **Sign out** — logs you out of the dashboard
- Footer: "Secured by Clerk"

### Authentication

- Sign-in is handled by Clerk (SSO)
- Account management (password, email, etc.) is through Clerk's hosted page

### Dashboard URL

- The organizer dashboard is at **hub.veent.io**

### General Notes

- All times in the dashboard use **Philippine Standard Time (GMT+8)**
- Supported currencies: **PHP** (Philippines) and **SGD** (Singapore)
- Maps use Leaflet + OpenStreetMap
- Rich text editors use Quill-style toolbar: Normal, bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, ordered list, bullet list, blockquote, code-block, link, clean
