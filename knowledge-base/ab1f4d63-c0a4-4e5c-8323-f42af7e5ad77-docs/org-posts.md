## Posts Tab

Access via: **Event Management Page > Posts tab**

The page has the heading "Posts" with the description: "Create and publish posts to be displayed at https://[subdomain].veent.net". There is a **+ Add Post** button.

### Creating a Post

Click **+ Add Post** to open the post drawer. The fields are:

- **Heading** — post title (text input, placeholder: "Add heading")
- **Image** — photo upload (placeholder: "Add post photo")
- **Post description** — rich text editor with toolbar: bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, ordered list, bullet list, blockquote, code-block, link, clean

Click **Save changes** to save the post, or **Cancel** to discard.

### Where Posts Appear

Posts are displayed on your event's public page at https://[subdomain].veent.net.
