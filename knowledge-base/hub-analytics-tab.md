## Analytics Tab — Sales & Revenue Data

### Accessing the Analytics Tab
Click the **Analytics** tab in the event navigation bar. This is the default page when you open an event.

### What You See on This Page

**Date Range Filter** (top) — filter analytics by date range (e.g., "Mar 15, 2026 - Mar 25, 2026")

**Quick Stats** — circle indicators showing key metrics at a glance

**Tickets by Type** section:
- Toggle between **Gross** and **Printed** views
- Shows a bar/table with each ticket type, price, sold count, and remaining count
- Example: "General Admission — ₱100.00 — Price: ₱100 — Remaining: 100/100"

**Gross Purchases by Ticket** section:
- Shows transaction fee and convenience fee status (e.g., "Absorbed Fee (7.0%): Absorbed", "Convenience Fee (₱20.00): Absorbed")
- Purchase data table

**Referrer Summary** section:
- Shows where traffic is coming from
- "Create a Referral Link" button to generate tracking links

**Voucher Summary** section:
- Shows voucher usage statistics

**Net Revenue Trend** section:
- Line chart showing revenue over time
