## Posts — Create, Edit, and Manage Posts

### How to Access Posts
Click the **Posts** tab in the event navigation bar.

### What You See
- Heading: "Posts" with note: "Create and publish posts to be displayed at https://[subdomain].veent.net"
- **+ Add Post** button
- List of existing posts (if any)

### How to Create a Post
1. Click the **Posts** tab
2. Click **+ Add Post** button
3. A drawer/panel slides open on the right with these fields:
   - **Heading** — the post title (text field)
   - **Image** — upload a post photo (click "Add post photo" to upload)
   - **Post description** — rich text editor for the post content
4. Click **Save changes** to publish the post
5. Click **Cancel** to discard

### How to Edit a Post
1. Click the **Posts** tab
2. Find the post you want to edit
3. Click on the post or its edit button
4. The same drawer opens with the post's current content
5. Make your changes
6. Click **Save changes**

### How to Delete a Post
1. Click the **Posts** tab
2. Find the post you want to delete
3. Click the delete option on that post
4. Confirm the deletion

### Where Do Posts Appear?
Posts are displayed on your event's public page at https://[subdomain].veent.net
