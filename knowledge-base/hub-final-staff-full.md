## Staff — Managing Team Members

### Staff Table
The Staff tab shows a table with columns:
- **Name** — staff member's name
- **Email** — their email address
- **Role** — their assigned role (MANAGER, EDITOR, VIEWER, or SCANNER)
- **Actions** — management options for that staff member

### Staff Roles and What They Can Do

| Role | Access Level |
|------|-------------|
| **Manager** | Full access to all event features — can edit everything, manage staff, access settings, send emails |
| **Editor** | Can edit content, manage tickets/vouchers/forms, and check in attendees. Cannot access settings or send emails |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to event information — can see data but cannot change anything |
| **Scanner** | Check-in only — can scan QR codes at the event but has no access to the dashboard |

### How to Remove or Change a Staff Member's Role
1. Click the **Staff** tab
2. Find the staff member in the table
3. Use the **Actions** column to manage them (change role or remove access)

### Important Notes
- The event creator (owner) is always listed as **MANAGER** and cannot be removed
- Staff members need a Veent account with the same email address you invited them with
- If a staff member can't log in, they need to sign up for a Veent account first using the same email
- Staff roles are per-event — the same person can have different roles in different events
- To invite staff to multiple events, you need to invite them separately to each event
