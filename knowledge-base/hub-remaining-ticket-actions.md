## Ticket Actions — Edit and Delete

### How to Access Ticket Options
1. Click the **Registration** tab
2. Find the ticket you want to manage in the **Tickets** section (left side)
3. Click the **three-dot menu** (⋮) on that ticket — it says "Open menu" when you hover

### Ticket Menu Options
When you click the three-dot menu on a ticket, you get two options:
- **Edit Ticket** — opens the ticket edit dialog where you can change the name, price, quantity, dates, and all other ticket settings
- **Delete Ticket** — removes the ticket permanently (be careful — this can't be undone)

### Editing a Ticket
1. Click the **Registration** tab
2. Find the ticket → click the **three-dot menu** (⋮)
3. Click **Edit Ticket**
4. The edit dialog opens with the same fields as the Create Ticket dialog:
   - Ticket Name, Price, Quantity, Image, Description, Status
   - Min Order, Max Order
   - Sales Start, Sales End (date + time)
   - Display options (show times)
   - Ticket Color
   - Display Settings (Package Ticket toggle)
5. Make your changes
6. Click **Save** to update the ticket

### Deleting a Ticket
1. Click the **Registration** tab
2. Find the ticket → click the **three-dot menu** (⋮)
3. Click **Delete Ticket**
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted
