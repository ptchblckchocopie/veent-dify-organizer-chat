## Staff & Scanning Areas Tab

### Accessing the Staff Tab
Click the **Staff** tab in the event navigation bar.

### What You See on This Page
The Staff tab has two sections:

1. **Staff List** (top) — heading "Staff List" with subtitle "Your Staff History"
2. **Scanning Areas** (bottom) — heading "Scanning Areas" with subtitle "Manage scanning areas for check-ins"

### Staff Management

**Staff list table columns:**
- Name
- Email
- Role (MANAGER, EDITOR, VIEWER, or SCANNER)
- Actions

**To invite a new staff member:**
1. Click the **Staff** tab
2. Click the **Invite Staff** button (red button, top right of Staff List section)
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role: Manager, Editor, Viewer, or Scanner
5. Click **Send Invite**

**Staff roles:**
- **Manager** — Full access to all event features
- **Editor** — Can edit content and manage tickets/vouchers/forms
- **Scanner** — Check-in only (scanning QR codes at the event)
- **Viewer** — Read-only access

### Scanning Areas

**To create a new scanning area:**
1. Click the **Staff** tab
2. Scroll down to the **Scanning Areas** section
3. Click the **New Scanning Area** button (red)
4. Name the area (e.g., "Main Entrance", "VIP Gate")
5. Click **Create Area**

**Scanning Areas section has two sub-tabs:**
- **Areas** — list of created scanning areas
- **For Approval** — scanner devices waiting to be approved

**To search scanning areas:**
Use the search bar in the Scanning Areas section
