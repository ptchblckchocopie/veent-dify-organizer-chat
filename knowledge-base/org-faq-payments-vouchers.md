## FAQ — Payments & Vouchers

### Payment & Fees

**Q: Which payment methods are supported?**
A: Maya (PHP) and Stripe (SGD/USD). The gateway is determined by event currency.

**Q: Who pays the transaction fees?**
A: You choose in Settings. "Pass On Transaction Fee" makes buyers pay, otherwise you absorb it.

**Q: How long do customers have to pay?**
A: Default is 15 minutes. Adjust "Reservation Expiry" in Settings.

**Q: What happens if payment fails?**
A: The order times out, reserved tickets are released, and the customer can try again.

### Vouchers

**Q: How do single-use vouchers work?**
A: The system generates unique codes with your prefix. Each code works exactly once.

**Q: Can I limit vouchers to specific tickets?**
A: Yes, select "Applicable Ticket Types" when creating the voucher.

**Q: How do I create an invite-only event?**
A: Enable "Require Voucher Code" in Settings and create vouchers for your invitees.

**Q: Can vouchers make tickets free?**
A: Yes, percentage or fixed discounts can reduce price to ₱0.
