## FAQ — Staff, Check-in & Emails

### Staff & Access

**Q: How do I add team members?**
A: Go to Staff page, click "Invite Staff", enter their email and select a role.

**Q: Can staff access multiple events?**
A: Yes, invite them separately to each event. Roles are per-event.

**Q: What can Editors do?**
A: Edit content, manage tickets/vouchers/forms, and check in attendees. They cannot access settings or send emails.

### Check-in

**Q: How do I set up check-in?**
A: Create Scanning Areas, assign stations (scanner devices), approve devices, and start scanning.

**Q: Does the scanner work offline?**
A: Yes, scans are queued and synced when connection is restored.

**Q: Can attendees check in multiple times?**
A: By default, no. Enable "Multiple Scan" on the scanning area to allow re-entry.

### Emails

**Q: When are confirmation emails sent?**
A: Automatically after successful payment or free order completion.

**Q: Can I customize confirmation emails?**
A: Yes, edit the email template under Emails. Use variables like `{{firstName}}`.

**Q: How do I resend a confirmation email?**
A: Go to Registrants, find the attendee, and click "Resend Email".
